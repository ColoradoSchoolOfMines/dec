2020 Summer Camp

EXPLORE Tech: Computer Science

For girls and boys entering 6th - 8th grade

In this camp, participants will create their very own projects by working with Bootstrap, a programming language that is perfect for beginners and experienced coders alike! Bootstrap combines mathematical concepts with common programming principles to develop games, graphics, and much more! By the end of this camp, participants will design, develop, test, troubleshoot, and present a final visual project!
