2017 Summer Camps and Workshops

Registration for both programs is now open!

EXPLORE Tech: Robotics

Robotics is all about FUN and creativity! Through robots, we learn about science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM!) in a hands-on and engaging way. This summer we are working with robots in a differentiated classroom that accommodates multiple levels and experience (Summer 2016 Campers: Welcome back!). Join us for a self-paced, fully supported adventure in Computational Thinking with Robotics!

Two Dates

  • Option 1: June 5th - 9th, 2017
  • Option 2: July 31st – August 4th, 2017

Separate sessions for girls and boys entering 6th - 9th grade in Fall 2017

  • Session A: for girls (taught by Mines female CS students)
  • Session B: for boys (taught by Mines male CS students)

TIME: 9:00am-3:30pm

COST: $400

Camp Features

  • Design an interactive visual application that interfaces with a robot
  • Learn visual programming environments
  • Eat lunch in the Colorado School of Mines dining hall
  • Enjoy all-session outdoor breaks and games
  • Learn CS fundamentals through interactive games and activities
