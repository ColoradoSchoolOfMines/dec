2019 Summer Camp

EXPLORE Tech: Computer Science

For girls and boys entering 6th - 9th grade

Innovating creative solutions to everyday problems is a key element in the growing field of Computer Science. These solutions can extend beyond the form of blocks of code to utilizing physical objects in unique and interactive ways. During this camp, participants will form connections between computer programming and everyday objects using the Makey Makey invention kit, a tool that can transform common items into a functioning keyboard, a controller, or any interactive system. Camp participants will use creativity to develop their own inventions!
