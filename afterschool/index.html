At Local Schools, Organizations, and Events

Our students provide after school programs at local area schools. If you would like to talk with us about administering a program at your school, please email us at stem-tech@mines.edu.

Our Mines students also attend local science fairs and STEM events when requested by schools and organizations. If you are interested in our program providing a display/activity/experiment at your next event, please contact us at the email above.
