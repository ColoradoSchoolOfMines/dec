EXPLORE TECH

For 7th and 8th grade girls, Fall 2016

New program offering this fall! Due to the popularity of our young girls’ program, DISCOVER TECH for grades 3-6, we are now offering continued programming for our middle school age group!

Girls can join us for exploration in EITHER CS (intro to programming with Scratch), or Physics (studying what we cannot sense), or BOTH!
  • Topics: Computer Science (CS) and Physics
  • Time: 5:00-6:15
  • Location: Colorado School of Mines campus
  • Cost: $60 (4-week session of CS OR Physics)*
  • OR $110 (8-week session of CS AND Physics)*
  • *Includes a program t-shirt

Exploration in Computer Science

Dip a toe into the world of computer programming with this 4-session course designed for beginners. Learn how to think like a programmer, solving problems creatively and efficiently using the visual programming language Scratch. Come ready to think outside the box!

Exploration in Physics

Many of the greatest discoveries in physics are the discoveries of those things in nature we can’t necessarily see... or hear... or touch! Come with us on a journey into the concept of discovery; how do we study things we cannot necessarily sense?
