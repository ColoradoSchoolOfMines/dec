2020 Fall Session

Girls can join us for exploration in EITHER Bootstrap:Algebra or Bootstrap:Reactive, or BOTH!

Bootstrap:Algebra

Prerequisite: none

Course Description: In this session, participants will create their very own projects by working with Bootstrap:Algebra, a programming environment combining mathematical concepts with common programming principles. It is perfect for beginners and experienced coders alike! Participants will use the programming language Racket to develop games, graphics, and much more! DECtech instructors will lead small Bootstrap Zoom lessons and participants will apply their knowledge by working through self-guided activities and projects at home. By the end of this 4-week session, participants will design, develop, test, troubleshoot, and present a final visual project.

Bootstrap: Reactive

Prerequisite: Bootstrap: Algebra or programming experience with a text-based language

Course Description: In this session, participants will build off concepts from Bootstrap:Algebra while exploring new ideas like data structures and conditional logic. DECtech instructors will lead small Bootstrap Zoom lessons and participants will apply their knowledge by working through self-guided activities and projects at home. We will be learning and using a programming language called Pyret to develop animations. By the end of our 4-week session, participants will design, develop, test, troubleshoot and present a re-creation of a classic video game!
