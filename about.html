Studies show that girls' interest in science/engineering subjects starts to decline the closer the girls get to middle school. In Fall 2012, Dr. Tracy Camp and her female Computer Science students created a program targeting the 3rd-6th grade age group. Discovering Technology is designed to foster and continue girls' interest in the STEM subjects through creative and interactive activities. The program is very popular with local elementary students, and has grown to include middle and high school aged girls.

On January 18, 2016, Dr. Camp received the MLK Recognition Award for her commitment to diversity and, specifically, the Discovering Technology program. Read more about Dr. Camp here.
